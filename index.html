Who We Are
The One-Sentence Answer
LVUX is a free, professional community for UX designers, researchers, and product thinkers building the future of experience design ‚Äî right here in Las Vegas.
Use this framing every time someone asks "what is LVUX?" ‚Äî in a caption, an email, a conversation at the event. It's accurate, it respects the audience's intelligence, and it doesn't oversell.
What LVUX Is
A professional community. We are practitioners ‚Äî designers, researchers, product managers, and strategists ‚Äî who take the craft seriously.

A monthly meetup. Real talks, real critique, real networking. Not a happy hour with a UX label on it.

Local and proud. Las Vegas is one of the world's most experience-driven economies. Every casino floor, resort app, and loyalty platform is a UX challenge at scale. We're building the community that matches that ambition.

Free. Always. No paywalls, no membership fees. The only cost is showing up.
What LVUX Is Not
Not a casual hangout. We're welcoming and fun ‚Äî but we lead with expertise, not vibes.

Not just for seniors. Students and career-changers belong here too, as long as they're serious about the work.

Not a job board. Networking is a bonus, not the product.

Not exclusive to designers. PMs, engineers, and strategists who care about UX are part of this community too.
Our Tone & Personality
We are confident but not arrogant. We speak like professionals who know their craft ‚Äî not like a brand trying to sound cool.
We are direct. No filler. No fluff. Every word in a post should earn its place.
We are grounded in Vegas. We embrace the city ‚Äî its scale, its ambition, its underdog reputation in the design world. We don't pretend to be San Francisco.
We are inclusive without being vague. Warmth and expertise coexist here.
We sound like A respected senior designer you'd grab coffee with
We don't sound like A corporate HR newsletter or a hype account
Energy Sharp. Warm. Purposeful.
Writing style Short sentences. Active voice. No jargon.
