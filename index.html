Who We Are
& How We Sound
When Someone Asks "What's LVUX?"
Las Vegas's UX community. Free monthly meetups for designers, researchers, and product thinkers who are serious about the craft.
That's it. No elevator pitch. No oversell. Vegas runs on instinct and confidence ‚Äî your answer should too. If they want more, they'll ask. And when they do, you've got this whole playbook.
What LVUX Is
A practitioner's community. Designers, researchers, PMs, and strategists who treat UX as a discipline ‚Äî not a job title. The kind of people who have opinions about cognitive load at dinner.

Monthly. In person. Real. One night a month, no Zoom link, no virtual background. Talks, critiques, and conversations that actually go somewhere.

Vegas, through and through. This city has been designing experiences at scale for a century. Every casino floor, resort app, loyalty platform, and kiosk is a live UX case study. We're the community built to match that ambition.

Free. Full stop. No membership tier, no premium access, no catch. Show up and contribute.
What LVUX Is Not
Not a networking mixer. Connections happen here, but they're a byproduct of doing good work together ‚Äî not the pitch.

Not a beginner's club. Newcomers are welcome, but the conversation doesn't dumb down for anyone. Come ready to engage.

Not a job board. Post openings on LinkedIn. Bring your work here.

Not exclusive to one discipline. If you touch the user experience ‚Äî whether you're a researcher, engineer, PM, or strategist ‚Äî you belong in the room.
Our Tone & Personality
Opinionated. We have a point of view on design, on Vegas, on what a community should actually do. We're not afraid to say so. Think AIGA Journal ‚Äî not a press release.
Specific. "Good vibes" is not a value proposition. Name the speaker. Name the topic. Name the street. Vegas Weekly doesn't describe the Strip as "exciting" ‚Äî it tells you exactly which table has the best odds.
Locally fluent. We know this city. We know Downtown is different from the Strip, which is different from Henderson. That specificity is what separates us from any other design meetup.
Warm without being soft. We welcome everyone. We lower the bar for nobody. Those two things coexist just fine.
We sound like A veteran Vegas designer who's shipped real work and has the receipts
We don't sound like A brand trying to seem relatable, or a meetup that's really just a recruiting event
Energy Direct. Local. Earned.
Writing style Specific, not vague. Confident, not loud. Vegas, not generic.
